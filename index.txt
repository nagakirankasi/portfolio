# about

About me

With over 20+ years of experience in mainframe applications development and modernization, I combine deep technical expertise with strategic business insight to drive digital transformation. 

My passion lies in seamlessly migrating and optimizing on-premise mainframe workloads, helping organizations transition to modern platforms. I have successfully led onshore and offshore teams, delivering modernization solutions across industries such as Healthcare, Banking, Financial Services, and Capital Markets in both the UK and the USA. 

As a PMP-certified professional, I excel at managing complex projects, ensuring smooth transitions while enhancing performance and scalability. Beyond mainframe optimization, I explore cloud-native development, AI-driven automation, and emerging technologies to future-proof enterprise applications. 

My goal is to bridge legacy systems with modern innovations, enabling businesses to stay agile, efficient, and competitive. Let’s connect and explore how we can drive transformation together!
